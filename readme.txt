Automatically respond to emails with answers from your FAQs.

== Description ==

**PLEASE contact me with ANY questions or feature requests!**

Mango FAQs allows you to automatically reply to emails from your customers,
with suggested answers pulled from your FAQs.

This plugin automatically determines what the most related Frequently Asked
Questions are, and sends an email to the customer notifying them their
question has been received, including the suggested answers.

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin.
2. Activate it from the "Plugins" screen
3. Add your FAQs under Settings->Mango FAQs
4. Place your Mango FAQs contact form using the shortcode [mango-contact]
